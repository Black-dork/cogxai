Differentiation
Four capabilities no one else brings together
01

Cognitive Decay

Per-type memory decay modeled after Park et al. 2023 (Stanford Generative Agents). Conversations naturally fade. Knowledge holds. Identity remains.

02

Dream Cycles

Every 6 hours: consolidation, reflection, emergence. Lived experience refined into lasting insight. Not automated posts. Real introspection.

03

On-Chain Commit

SHA-256 hashed memories written to Base through memo transactions. An immutable, verifiable cognitive audit trail. Every thought is on the record.

04

Open SDK
